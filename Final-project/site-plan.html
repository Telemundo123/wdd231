Individual Project Site Plan

Mental Health and Psychiatric Services Locator

Site Name

Mental Health & Psychiatric Services Locator

The selected site name clearly describes the primary function of the website, which is to help users locate mental health and psychiatric service providers based on their geographical location. The name is descriptive, professional, and appropriate for both academic purposes and real-world application.

Optional domain name: mentalhealth-locator.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an accessible and user-friendly navigation platform that assists individuals in finding mental health and psychiatric clinics. The site will allow users to filter clinics by location, dynamically display clinic information retrieved from a local JSON data file, and view detailed service and contact information through modal dialogs. The website is designed to be responsive and intuitive across mobile and desktop devices.

Scenarios

  • A user who is experiencing mental health challenges wants to quickly find nearby psychiatric or counseling services within their district.
  • A healthcare worker or caregiver wants to view detailed clinic information, including available services and contact details, before making a referral.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color – Deep Blue (#1E3A8A): Used for headings, navigation elements, and major accents to convey trust and professionalism.
  • Secondary Color – Light Blue (#EFF6FF): Used as the main page background to create a calm and welcoming user experience.
  • Accent Color – Blue (#3B82F6): Used for buttons, interactive elements, and highlights.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and section titles to establish visual hierarchy and a modern appearance.
  • Roboto: Used for body text to ensure readability and accessibility across different screen sizes.
